Connect to Azure DevOps

The extension needs a Personal Access Token to talk to Azure DevOps. Create one at dev.azure.com → User settings → Personal access tokens with at least Work Items (Read & Write) and Project and Team (Read) scopes. The PAT is stored in chrome.storage.local on this machine only.

Paste a PAT and click Load to pick your organization and project from the dropdowns, or just type them in.
